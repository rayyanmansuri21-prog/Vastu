Introduction to Vastu

Vastu Shastra is the ancient Indian science of architecture and design, harmonizing natural energies to promote health, prosperity, and happiness. It integrates art, astronomy, and environmental balance to create spaces that feel positive and energetic.

Key Elements of Vastu

  • Direction: Placement based on cardinal directions (North, South, East, West).
  • Five Elements: Earth, Water, Fire, Air, and Space — each affects life differently.
  • Energy Flow: Ensuring positive energy circulation throughout the space.
  • Balance: Proper balance of materials, light, and design for harmony.

Direction Guide

Vastu assigns certain qualities and purposes to each direction. For example:

  • North: Wealth and prosperity
  • East: Knowledge and education
  • South: Stability and security
  • West: Social connections

Room Placements

  • Kitchen: Southeast
  • Master Bedroom: Southwest
  • Living Room: North or East
  • Bathrooms: Northwest or Southeast
  • Pooja/Prayer Room: Northeast

Quick Tips

  1. Keep Northeast clean and clutter-free.
  2. Ensure proper ventilation and natural light.
  3. Avoid heavy objects in the center of the house.
  4. Use natural colors for walls and furniture.
  5. Maintain harmony with nature — plants, water features, etc.

Benefits of Vastu-Compliant Spaces

  • Mental peace and positivity
  • Better health and energy levels
  • Improved business success and wealth
  • Family harmony and reduced conflicts
  • Enhanced creativity and productivity
